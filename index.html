CIAA ले नेपालको इतिहासकै सबैभन्दा ठूला भ्रष्टाचार मुद्दाहरू मध्ये एक मानिएको पोखरा क्षेत्रीय अन्तर्राष्ट्रिय विमानस्थल निर्माणमा ५५ व्यक्ति र एक कम्पनीविरुद्ध विशेष अदालतमा मुद्दा दर्ता गर्‍यो। विमानस्थल चीनको Exim Bank को USD $216 मिलियन ऋणमा निर्मित हो।

CIAA को दाबी: रु. ८.३६ अर्ब ($74 मिलियन) फुलाइएको लागत अनुमान, एकतर्फी बोलपत्र, र निर्माण निरीक्षणमा हेरफेरमार्फत अपचलन। NC वरिष्ठ नेता राम शरण महतलाई अर्थमन्त्री रहँदा (२०१६) अनियमित ऋण सम्झौता स्वीकृत गरेको आरोप छ।

संसदीय समितिको उपसमिति प्रतिवेदनले पहिले नै रु. १० अर्बको अनियमितता देखाउँदै अभियोजनको सिफारिस गरेको थियो। OCCRP ले यसलाई "नेपालको भ्रष्टाचारविरोधी प्रयासमा ऐतिहासिक कदम" भनेर वर्णन गर्‍यो।

NC Finance Minister Mahat Signed Disputed Chinese Loan Agreement for Pokhara Airport
China Exim Bank loan agreement signed March 21, 2016. CIAA later alleged the agreement itself enabled systematic corruption, inflated estimates, and Rs. 8.36B ($74M) in losses.
2021–2022
NC Home Minister Khand Allegedly Enabled Rs. 288M Bhutanese Refugee Fraud
While Khand served as Home Minister, 875 Nepali citizens were defrauded of Rs. 288 million via false promises of US resettlement through forged Bhutanese refugee documents.
May 2023
Khand Arrested. Deuba Defended Him. NC Kept Him on Central Committee.
Police arrested Khand (May 10–13). Court remanded him (June 16). NC President Deuba gave public defense. NC retained Khand as CWC member while UML immediately suspended its own accused leader. Arzoo Rana Deuba (NC MP) also under investigation.
Feb. 2024

Special Court convicted Rana (2 years + Rs. 848,000 fine). Former NC Deputy PM Gachhadar acquitted via "policy decision" argument. CIAA appealed to Supreme Court in 2025.
May 2025
NC Member Basnet Suspended from Parliament — Teramocs (Rs. 3.21B) Case Filed
CIAA filed major telecom procurement corruption case. NC member Basnet suspended from Parliament on May 15, 2025. The Himalayan Times: "Most prominent corruption prosecution of the year."
Dec. 7, 2025
NC Leader Mahat Among 55 Charged in One of Nepal's Largest-Ever Corruption Cases
CIAA filed Rs. 8.36B ($74M) Pokhara Airport corruption case against 55 individuals — including NC former Finance Minister Mahat. Confirmed by OCCRP, The Himalayan Times, and Fiscal Nepal.
